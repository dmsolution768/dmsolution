Hello, we are DM Solution

Established in 2017 as a trading firm, DM Solution has delivered satisfactory services to its more than 100 clients. "The Earth is what we all have in common." -with this vision, DM Solution committed to do its part for Environment and started manufacturing biodegradable papercups in 2019 using fully automatic machines with best quality production.


Company Profile:

DM Solution, as an Individual-proprietor firm, produces more than 50 Lacs Cups / Month with quality precision and rigorous testing.We also deal in paper cup raw material. Registered with GST No. 07EJWPM9591K1ZX, DM Solution accepts all mode of payment(Cash, Card, Cheque, NEFT etc) with Canara Bank as its trusted Banker.
